UniCredit Bank

UniCredit is an Italian global banking and financial services group. Its network spans 50 markets in 17 countries giving them one of Europe’s highest market shares. They were the official bank of UEFA club competitions in 2018, its final year as a major sponsor.

The Brief

—

They asked us to activate their sponsorship of leading UEFA club properties, including both the UEFA Europa League and prestigious UEFA Champions League. The goal was to leverage all facets of the partnership, with a key focus on their highly valued ticketing and sole & exclusive assets. The showpiece UEFA Champions League Final, one of the world’s greatest annual sporting events, also required a guest hospitality programme to be delivered in Kyiv, Ukraine.

The Process

—

My role was to spearhead a team of three in managing the ticketing programs across both competitions. This involved administration of the online portal and ongoing communication with branches across Europe to process initial requests and make ongoing changes up until kick off. I also coordinated the onsite distribution of ticketing and guest programs for the UEFA Champions League Final in Kyiv, Ukraine which required careful planning to overcome complex customs policies and general overseas shipping and travel logistics.

The Outcome

—

Over 18,000 tickets were distributed throughout the season to C-Suite executive and senior clients of the bank across 17 key European markets. The activity culminated in delivery of a seamless experience for over 500 VIP guests in Kyiv.
