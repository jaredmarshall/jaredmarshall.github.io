Jetstar Australia

Jetstar is an Australian low-cost airline headquartered in Melbourne and wholly owned by Qantas. They partnered with Little Athletics Australia (LAA) as a way to connect with families across Australia, a key customer segment for the carrier.

The Brief

—

They asked us to review their LAA partnership, focusing on how they can further amplify and evolve the Little Jetstars initiative. The task was to come up with fresh ideas across the key amplification pillars of PR, Content & Social to extend the reach beyond the LAA community.

The Process

—

My role was to spearhead the development of the creative concept and carry momentum through to the roll out the campaign nationally. I managed all planning and activity across a range of key areas including competition management, talent brokerage, community engagement, event logistics, social media and content creation.

The Outcome

—

The Little Jetstars, Big Heroes program was launched giving LAA participants around the country the chance to win exclusive training sessions with elite athletes from a range of sports. A total of 700 entries were received, with five winners selected. Fun, shareable content was crafted around the winner announcements and Big Hero experiences, reaching almost 2.7m people across owned channels.
