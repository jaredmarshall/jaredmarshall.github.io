AT-MATCH ACTIVATION

Cricket Australia

Cricket Australia is the governing body for professional cricket in Australia. They push the boundaries when it comes to engaging with both traditional cricket tragics and new fans to the game.

The Brief

—

They asked us to manage the at-match activation program across the summer of One Day Internationals. The key was to create a new social entertainment experience that would reset the perceptions of a typical day at the cricket. The ultimate goal was to attract millennials to the game.

The Process

—

My role was to lead the project from planning through to execution, liaising with key suppliers on everything from the creative build and styling to the catering and signage. It was the inaugural year for this concept which presented challenges but we were able to shape a unique experience for the fan from start to finish.

The Outcome

—

The Lawn, a custom built space, was brought to life and provided fans with a unique viewpoint along with a tailored food and beverage offering whilst enjoying the action. Over 150 guests enjoyed the experience and we were successful in creating an interest in the game, with most saying they would attend a match again when surveyed post event.
