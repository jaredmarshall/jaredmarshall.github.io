EVENT MANAGEMENT

International Swimming League

The International Swimming League (ISL) is a break-away professional competition established in 2019. The team-based, fast-paced league is changing the world of competitive swimming as we know it. The ISL is headquartered in Europe and hosts matches around the globe.

The Brief

—

They asked us to project manage the London match, a key market for them to capture first up. The goal was to deliver a best-in-class event that set the standard for the Final match in Las Vegas and for future series. We wanted new and old swimming fans to see the sport like never before and create an atmosphere so the world’s top athletes could perform at their best.

The Process

—

My role was to lead a team of 5 liaising with a range of internal and external stakeholders across key streams of venue operations, branding, ticketing, marketing, media & promotion, show production, fan engagement and athlete services. I was involved in all phases of the project lifecycle, from pitching for and winning the business to planning, organisation and implementation of the event.

The Outcome

—

A sea of 3,500 spectators covered the iconic London Aquatics Centre across the two days of short-course competition. 112 of the world’s best swimmers competed in the European derby match which was won by team Energy Standard. CSR and media activity amplified the match whilst increasing the wider awareness of the ISL. A premium hospitality experience was offered to VIP guests at match who enjoyed champagne and canapés from the best seats in the house. The event was highly praised and widely regarded as the best of the global series.
