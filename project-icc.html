BRAND ACTIVATION

International Champions Cup

The International Champions Cup (ICC) is an annual club association football exhibition competition. It features teams from Europe playing pre-season friendly matches. In the winter of 2015 and 2016 Australia hosted powerhouse clubs including Manchester City FC, Tottenham Hotspur FC and Juventus FC.

The Brief

—

They asked us to bring their brands to life by developing activation plans, increasing their presence at match and around town whilst they were here. The goal was to engage with and grow the global fanbase of each club by providing local spectators with experiences unique to each team.

The Process

—

My role across the three projects was to develop ideas for the activation, pitch the plans to the client and ultimately manage the delivery of each by working with various stakeholders. These included third-party technology, signage and infrastructure suppliers, venue and safety managers, ticketing providers and the clubs themselves.

The Outcome

—

The ‘CityOnTour’, ’SpursInOz’ and ‘JuveOnTour’ campaigns were promoted through immersive activation zones outside of the famous Melbourne Cricket Ground. Fans were encouraged to participate in skills clinics and challenges to win prizes, have their photo taken in booths to receive a branded takeaway, and print their very own banners to show messages of support. Live bands, face painting, club mascots and football tricksters added to the colour and excitement.
